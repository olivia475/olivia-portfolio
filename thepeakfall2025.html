The PEAK Fall 2025

Illustration Collection

A collection of original illustrations drawn for SFU's The Peak newspaper throughout the Fall 2025 semester.

Objective

I've always enjoyed drawing, but finding inspiration can be challenging sometimes.

Last year, I joined The Peak's mailing list but never made the time to contribute. However, this fall, I decided to finally dive in in hopes of incorporating a creative break from heavier coursework.

This became a fun side project that helped me reconnect with drawing over the last few months.

Illustrations

Takeaways

Throughout the semester, I experimented with new styles and digital drawing techniques. While digital art is still quite new to me (I'm more comfortable with traditional painting and sketching), this work gave me space to practice, improve, and start discovering my own style.

I plan to continue contributing illustrations in the spring, and I'm looking forward to seeing what creative headliners they write next!
